Key Points in Robotics

  1. Robotics is an interdisciplinary field involving engineering,computer science,and technology.
  2. Robots are used in various sectors like healthcare,industry,and space exploration.
  3. Industrial robots automate processes and improve proctivity and safety in industries.
  4. Humanoid robots can perfom task similar to humans and interact naturally with people.
  5. Advances in AI and machine learning are makin robots more autonomous and adaptive.
